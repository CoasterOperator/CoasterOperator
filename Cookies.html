Cookies Policy

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and provide you with a more personalized service. Below, you can learn more about the types of cookies we use and how you can manage your preferences.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences and improve website functionality.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be switched off. They enable core functionality, such as security and accessibility.

2. Analytical Cookies

Analytical cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information. This allows us to improve performance and content.

3. Marketing Cookies

Marketing cookies track your online activity to deliver targeted advertisements based on your browsing behavior.

Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.
